About this space
New York is more than just skyscrapers and fast lanes. Welcome to The Industrialist, where artists and renovated warehouses make for an exciting corner of Brooklyn. This four-story location embraces the industrial history of Union Street. Bold black brick facade. Square paneled windows. And micro-rooms with comfortable beds to lay your head at night.

Step outside to explore endless dining, shopping, and entertainment options. Lavender latte’s from Root Hill Cafe. Thrifty fashion finds at L Train Vintage. Ever heard of bouldering? Around here, we climb indoors at Brooklyn Boulders. Satisfy your hunger at Dinosaur Bar-B-Que with mouthwatering brisket nachos. Or stop by Strong Rope Brewery for a locally crafted beer with homegrown New York ingredients. At The Industrialist, stay at the center of Brooklyn’s dynamic culture and food scene.
